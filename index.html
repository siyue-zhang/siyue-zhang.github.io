I am a fourth-year PhD student with NTU AI Language Lab at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), supervised by Prof. Anh Tuan Luu. I am also visiting Prof. Chen Zhao's lab at New York University (NYU) Shanghai. Before joining NTU, I obtained my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from École Polytechnique and Bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University. My research broadly explores natural language processing, with interests in areas such as agentic search, information retrieval, and retrieval-augmented generation.
