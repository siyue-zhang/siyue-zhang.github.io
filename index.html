I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the NTU AI Language Lab at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), supervised by Prof. Anh Tuan Luu. I am also a visiting researcher in Chen Zhao's lab at NYU Shanghai. Before joining NTU, I obtained my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from École Polytechnique and my B.S. degree from Zhejiang University. My research broadly explores natural language processing, with interests in areas such as agentic search, embedding models, and retrieval-augmented generation.
